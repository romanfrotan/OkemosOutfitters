About Okemos Outfitters

Hello and welcome to Roman's Outfitters, we are a small family owned company from Okemos,MI we are committed to providing the best fishing gear and an affordable price. we specialize in targeting freshwater species, we have a quality selection of traditional spin rods and reels as well as high quality fly-fishing equipment to efficiency fish the local rivers. We offer to price match any product you wish to order from outside vendors just please fill out the "Add Outsourced gear and Tackle" form from the main homepage, and we will be sure to get your specialized product in our store!
